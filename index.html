About Us

BACKGROUND

Ludewa Development Group (LUDEG) is the registered Community Based Organization (CBO) with registration number LDC/GR/925 and its head quarter in Ludewa town.

It is the active organization that promotes and assists development within Ludewa district in education, community health, social welfare and economic issues that support community so as to alleviate poverty.

OUR PROGRAMMES

LUDEG has linked with professional and experienced personnel in the field of Agricultural sciences, Food technology, Agricultural economics and rural development. It has already established active cooperation with other individuals, CBO’s, NGO’s, FBO’s and Government institutions within Ludewa district in action to foster the development process.

LUDEG by contract may partner with these organizations to conduct research survey, Agribusiness value chain development, Project proposal writing, Project Report writing, Statistical data analysis and presentations and providing knowledge based education regarding project development

MISION STATEMENT

1. Promoting health and social well-being of district residents.

2. To promote food availability quality and security.

3. To fight against poverty through agricultural and livestock produce marketing.

4. To promote Environmental conservation.

5. Promoting sports, Community recreation and play facilities.

6. To raise funds for community livelihood improvement in agriculture, livestock keeping, education, health and environment.

7. To promote value added products.

8. Work with similar groups within and out of the district.
